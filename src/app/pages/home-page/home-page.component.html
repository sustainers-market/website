A market for the common good

The Sustainer's Market is a sustainable way to pay for app subscriptions and digital services. Businesses on the Market earn only what they need, and you earn when there's more than enough to sustain them.

A business that runs on the Sustainer's Market decides how much money is needed to sustain its service and pay its team. Any payment you make to a business will contribute to its sustainability.

Once a business earns enough to become sustainable, any extra earnings are rewarded back to its customers as a refund, or paid out as dividends to people who spend on the Market.

This economy offers businesses a path to sustainable growth, and gives customers the opportunity to be among the most rewarded shareholders.
