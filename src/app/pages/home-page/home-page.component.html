Sustainability as a service

The Sustainer's Market is a sustainable way to fund projects like open source services or online subscriptions. Projects on the Market earn only what they need, and the rest is returned to you.

A Sustainer's Market project publishes how much money is needed to run its service and pay its team in a Sustainability Report. Any payment you make to a project will contribute to its sustainability.

Once a project earns enough to become sustainable, any extra earnings are returned back to its sustainers as a dividend.

This economy offers projects a path to sustainable and transparent growth, while being a trustworthy way for people to support work they care about.

If you have a project you'd like to run on the Sustainers market, reach out to us and we'll help you get started.
