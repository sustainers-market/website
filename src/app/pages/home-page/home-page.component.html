A place to shop mindful businesses

Introducing a sustainable way to pay for digital services. A Sustainer Network account is free and lets you log in to any service on the network.

Sign into any sustainable service using your Sustainer Network account, and use the service normally .
Any payment you make to a service will go through the Network. You can log in here any time to see which services you're paying for and how much.
Once a service grows enough to reach sustainability (its revenue is enough to cover its expenses) any profit is rewarded back to its users as a refund, or reinvested into the network as needed.
To maintain transparency, sustainable services keep their revenue and expenses visible on their sustainernetwork.org page.
