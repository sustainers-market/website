A market for the
common good

The Sustainer's Market is a sustainable way to pay for services. Businesses on the Market earn only what they need, and you earn when there's more than enough to sustain them.

A business that is part of the Sustainer's Market decides how much money is needed to sustain its service and pay its team. Any payment you make to a business will contibute to its sustainability.
Once a business earns enough to cover its expenses and payouts, any extra earnings are rewarded back to its users as a refund, or to the Market for paying out dividends to participants.
It's in the best interest of a businesses to prioritize customer service and be transparent about its costs, because it is you, the customers, who are choosing to use and sustain the service. The Sustainer's Market provides a platform to make this easy.
